About

I'm a third-year graduate student at the College of Computer Science and Technology of Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen). My research interest lies in Privacy Preservation in Machine Learning. My current graduation research is around privacy preservation in Hierarchical Federated Learning, focusing on Differential Privacy and Secure Aggregation. My advisor is Prof. Weizhe Zhang.

Welcome to discuss and explore the related research with me!

I received my Bechelor degree of Information Security from Xidian University in July 2019.
I will receive my Master degree of Computer Science and Technology from HIT in December 2021.
I will pursuit my Doctor degree of Computer Science and Technology at HIT in March 2022.

I like running, swimming, playing logical reasoning games, and watching movies in my spare time.
